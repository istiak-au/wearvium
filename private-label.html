Private label & OEM / ODM.

Build your own label, or develop from a tech pack or an early concept. We take it through fabric, fit, trims, branding, and packaging — and into production.

Your label, developed at the source.

Whether you arrive with a finished tech pack or a rough idea on a moodboard, we turn it into a made garment — sampled, corrected, and produced to a documented standard.

You keep the brand and the design; we bring the development discipline and the manufacturing base to make it real, at a volume that grows with you.

Three ways to engage

Private label, OEM, ODM

Private label
We produce to your brand and your specification — your label, your packaging, our manufacturing and quality control.
OEM
You bring the design or tech pack; we manufacture to it, managing fabric, trims, fit, and finishing.
ODM
Start from a concept and let us develop the design and pattern with you, then produce the finished range.
