From brief to delivery.

Six connected steps keep one team accountable the whole way — so there are no surprises between your brief and the finished order.

01

Brief review

We confirm category, target use, quantity, timeline, and any compliance or branding requirements — so the scope is clear before anything is made.

02

Development alignment

We agree specifications, fabric, trims, labels, and packaging, and document them as the standard the order will be measured against.

03

Sampling

Concept becomes a physical sample with a clear, documented approval path — corrections are made and signed off before production.

04

Production

Approved samples go into production through vetted factory partners, with progress visible so you can plan around it.

05

Quality control

Garments are checked against the agreed specification for fabric, fit, and finishing before anything ships.

06

Delivery

We coordinate documentation and global delivery, and set up managed reorders where the program needs them.
